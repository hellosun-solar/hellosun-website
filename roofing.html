Your roof is the foundation
of everything built on it.

Most solar companies aren't roofing companies. We are. Deep roots in roofing mean we treat every penetration, flashing detail, and mount point the right way—like roofers do, not like solar installers hope to get away with.

What We Hear. What We Do.

The roof problems solar companies create — and how we avoid them.

Most solar installers aren't roofers. You can tell.

Installing racking isn't the same as proper flashing, penetration integration, or knowing how different roofing materials work. Work that passes inspection can fail later when details aren't right.

HelloSun

We have hands-on roofing experience. We understand how different roofing systems work at penetration points—asphalt, metal, low-slope membranes. That knowledge goes into every system we install, whether we installed the roof or not.

Two contractors means nobody owns the problem.

When your solar company and roofer are different, nobody fully owns the connection between them. When something leaks, the solar company blames the roofer. The roofer blames the penetrations. You're stuck waiting for answers.

HelloSun

When HelloSun installs both roof and solar, we cover them together under one contract. We guarantee the combination we installed won't leak. One company. One guarantee. No gaps.

Cheap installers use deck mounts. Fast isn't right.

Direct-to-deck mounts drill straight into the deck with just sealant—no flashing, no integration with the roof system. Sealant degrades. These mounts haven't been tested for decades of real conditions: freeze-thaw, UV, thermal movement. The industry went fast and skipped the tests.

HelloSun

We only use mechanically flashed mounts. Every attachment point integrates with the roofing system—like a pipe vent or skylight—the way proper roofs have been done for decades. We won't use unproven products just to save a few man-hours. We won't make that tradeoff.

A failing roof with solar panels is expensive.

If your roof fails after solar is installed, every panel comes off first. Removing and reinstalling panels adds thousands to an otherwise cheaper re-roof.

HelloSun

We assess your roof before we start. If it needs work, we address it as part of the project—one team, one mobilization, no surprises later.
