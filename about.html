HelloSun exists because the solar industry failed. Pennsylvania homeowners and businesses deserve better. We built the company we wanted to exist.

Why We Exist

The solar industry grew fast.
Not always right.

Solar panels work. But the industry hasn't. Most "solar companies" are really sales organizations—sales reps who get paid commissions to close deals and hand your project to the cheapest installer. The results? Poor work, unhappy customers, inflated projections, and a company that's gone before you can react. Accountability doesn't matter when the business model is about the sale, not the customer.

We built HelloSun to be the opposite. We're built on truth. We'll give you real numbers. If your property isn't a good fit, we say so. We won't sacrifice integrity to close a deal. Our mission is real energy infrastructure—and standing behind our work long after install. No inflated promises. No pressure. No disappearing act.

Our Background

Built from service,
skill, and purpose.

I'm Keith Sowell, founder of HelloSun. I'm a U.S. Marine Corps veteran and Pennsylvania Army National Guard member. My military career took me into aerospace and aviation — environments where quality procedures aren't suggestions and checklists exist for a reason. When something fails, there is no ambiguity about who is responsible. That's the standard I grew up in professionally, and it's the only one I know how to operate by.

After aviation, I spent years in the roofing and solar industry. That experience gave me a ground-level understanding of how roof and solar systems are actually built — and where most solar companies create problems they never come back to fix.

I started HelloSun because I saw that the issues in the solar industry weren't caused by the technology, they were institutional. Solar technology really works and actually helps people when it's done the right way. HelloSun was built for that purpose. We are not a high-volume sales organization. We're an energy infrastructure company. We sell it, we design it, we install it, and we stand behind it. Our mission is simple: to give you choice, control, and a system that performs for decades. We don't measure success by how many deals we close. We measure it by how well our systems perform — and how well we serve the families who trust us.

Keith Sowell Founder, HelloSun
