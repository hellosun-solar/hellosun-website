Battery Storage

The grid is under more stress
than it's ever been.

EV charging, AI data centers, electrification of everything — the grid wasn't built for what it's being asked to do. Outages are more frequent. Peak rates are higher. Solar without storage only solves half the problem. Batteries solve the rest.

What We Hear. What We Do.

Real concerns. Direct answers.

The power goes out and your solar system shuts off too.

Grid-tied solar without battery backup automatically disconnects when the grid goes down — by code, for safety. So when the lights go out, so does your solar. You're left in the dark either way.

HelloSun

Battery storage keeps your critical circuits running through an outage. We size the system around the loads that actually matter to you — and make sure it works when you need it.

A generator works — until it doesn't.

Generators run on fuel. In a short outage that's fine. In a serious, extended emergency — a major storm, a regional grid failure — fuel gets scarce fast. They also require maintenance, produce exhaust, make noise, and need to be manually started and fueled around the clock.

HelloSun

A solar-paired battery runs silently, requires no fuel, and recharges itself every day the sun rises. Clouds slow it down occasionally — but the sun has never stopped showing up. In a long-term scenario, a battery system is the more resilient choice by a wide margin.

Solar reduces your bill. Storage lets you own your energy.

A grid-tied solar system still makes you a utility customer. You still depend on their infrastructure, their pricing, their reliability, and their rules. That's energy reduction — not energy independence.

HelloSun

Pair solar with storage and you produce, store, and control your own power. Your home or business runs on what you generate — not what the utility decides to charge you for.

Already have solar? Adding storage later is an option.

Many homeowners installed solar before battery costs came down enough to make sense. Now the math has changed — and adding storage to an existing system is often simpler than starting from scratch.

HelloSun

We assess your existing system and design a storage solution that integrates cleanly. Inverter compatibility, load analysis, proper permitting — handled correctly from the start.
