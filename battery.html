Battery Storage

The grid is under stress.
It's only getting worse.

EV charging, AI data centers, electrification—the grid wasn't built for this demand. Outages are more frequent. Peak rates are higher. Solar alone solves half the problem. Batteries solve the rest.

What We Hear. What We Do.

Real concerns. Direct answers.

Your solar shuts off when the grid goes down.

Grid-tied solar automatically disconnects when the grid fails—for safety. So when the power goes out, your solar goes out too. You're in the dark either way.

HelloSun

Battery storage keeps your critical circuits running during an outage. We size the system for what matters to you and make sure it works when you need it.

Generators work—until you run out of fuel.

In a short outage, generators are fine. In a long emergency—bad storm, grid failure—fuel gets hard to find fast. Plus they need maintenance, produce exhaust, make noise, and need constant manual attention.

HelloSun

A solar-paired battery runs silently, needs no fuel, and recharges itself every sunny day. Clouds slow it down occasionally—but the sun always comes back. In a long emergency, batteries are far more reliable.

Solar cuts your bill. Storage gives you control.

A grid-tied solar system still makes you a utility customer. You still depend on their infrastructure, pricing, and rules. That's energy reduction—not independence.

HelloSun

Pair solar with storage and you produce, store, and control your own power. Your home or business runs on what you generate—not utility charges.

Already have solar? Adding storage later is an option.

Many homeowners installed solar before battery costs came down enough to make sense. Now the math has changed — and adding storage to an existing system is often simpler than starting from scratch.

HelloSun

We assess your existing system and design a storage solution that integrates cleanly. Inverter compatibility, load analysis, proper permitting — handled correctly from the start.
